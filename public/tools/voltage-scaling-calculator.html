Formula (matches the spreadsheet):
Signal → Scaled: ((sMax − sMin) × (reading − vMin)) ÷ (vMax − vMin) + sMin
Scaled → Signal: ((vMax − vMin) × (reading − sMin)) ÷ (sMax − sMin) + vMin
Both are linear interpolations between the two ranges and are exact inverses of each other.
